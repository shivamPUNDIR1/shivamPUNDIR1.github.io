Sushiksha- The Flagship Program of T.O.G is in true senses the heart and soul of our organization.

With over 85 students under our umbrella, we not only believe in imparting proper education but the holistic development of the students. We make whole hearted efforts to make sure the students go through properly curated lessons on Mannerism, Hygiene and Sanitation.

Most of our students are below 15 years are getting formal education from government schools but we all know how those schools are. We divide the students into different groups and sections based upon their skills and pre-requisite knowledge to make sure we have alike minds in a certain group,

Sushiksha bridges the gap between the private education and government education standards of our country, currently at a smaller scale.

We make sure the non-attending school students are enrolled for formal education and the team helps them over the top with their education. We in true senses believe that we are the silent torch bearers for the Right to Education scheme by being behind the curtains and helping the students not only with their textbook learning but life learnings too. We celebrate almost each festival known to us to inculcate the spirit of secularism and nationalism in the students. We make sure that they get the right amount of social and moral values which will help them in the outer world. We have students from all backgrounds and religions learning how peace and harmony go hand in hand.

With this believe, we go into the ground each day to make sure, every dream comes true.



80 students from various of age groups and diverse backgrounds attend classes, taught by our on ground team in the Shri 1008 Munisuvrat Nath Digamber Jain Mandir in Sector 15 Part 1, Gurugram. These classes are conducted between 4pm and 5:30pm on all days. Our main motive behind this program is to uplift the underprivileged families by imparting knowledge to the young ones and making them socially responsible so that the future generations can play a vital role in the holistic development of their families and also of our country.
